Thank you much for visiting my webiste! My name is Ben Reilly and I am a software engineer who does full-stack web development.

I have many interests, but I at the moment I enjoy making things with and learning about human computer interaction, experimental user interfaces, machine learning (especailly natural langauge processing), and generative art. These are subjects where one doesn't need to go to deep to experience the magical, uncanny, and deeply weird phenomena of computers and the internet.

I like to think of internet, even the seemingly cold, mechanical, or commercial parts of it, as a massive communal artistic project we are all doing together. Kind of like a city; any scene or moment within it becomes a work of art as soon as you snap a photo of it. I think code is a tool but it is also a material, a medium in it's own right. We use it to make things, but they can have a sort of life of their own. It is a great simulacrum, a self-prtrait of huamnity, an as such, and opportunity to see ourselves and learn some things;)
